HangGame tutorial!

Here's the scoop: The objective is to guess what word fills the blank spaces below. If you can't guess ask simpsons a hint

  1. Click on a key of your guess to start.
  2. If the letter is contained in the word(s), it will display in the word board below OR if not part of the hangman will make the sound to alert you, and the letter you chose will go to the bottom as missed letters.
  3. Reset and repeat until either you guessed the whole word or the man is hung [heh, heh].
